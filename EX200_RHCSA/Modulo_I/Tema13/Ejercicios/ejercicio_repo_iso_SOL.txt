INSTALAR UN REPOSITORIO DE LA ISO WORKSTATION
---------------------------------------------
En ServerX

0. Obtener la iso de rhel72_workstation de content/rhel72_workstation/
 
   wget -O /tmp/rhel-workstation-7.2-x86_64-dvd.iso http://content/rhel72_workstation/rhel-workstation-7.2-x86_64-dvd.iso

1.- Montar la iso de workstation en /rhel72_workstation
    
    mkdir /rhel72_workstation
    mount -o loop /tmp/rhel-workstation-7.2-x86_64-dvd.iso /rhel72_workstation

2.- Crear un repositorio que se llame workstation

     vim /etc/yum.repos.d/workstation.repo
       [workstation]
       name= paquetes de la iso de workstation
       baseurl=file:///rhel72_workstation
       enabled=1
       gpgckeck=0
 
 Comprobar con: yum repolist que aparece

4.- Desactivar el repositorio workstation

    poner a enabled=0 en el fichero /etc/yum.repo.d/workstation.repo
   Comprobar con: yum repolist que ya no sale

5. Desmontar la iso
   umount /rhel72_workstation
